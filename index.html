All about IP:

IP address is known as Internet Protocol. It is a unique address that identifies the device on the internet or the local network. IP adresses are the identifier that allow local information to be sent between devices on network.

These are not just some random numbers but are assigned by the Internet Assigned Numbers Authority (IANA), a division of the Internet Corporation for Assigned Names and Numbers (ICANN).All devices find, send, and exchange information with other connected devices using this protocol. By speaking the same language, any computer in any location can talk to one another
